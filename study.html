Conclusion

The information provided suggests a balanced distribution of dry and oily skin types, with oily skin slightly more prevalent (52.2%) than dry skin (47.8%), as depicted in a pie chart. The suitability of skincare products is categorized in a bar graph, with red lines indicating products for oily skin, blue lines for dry skin, and red and blue lines for combination skin. The price range for these products spans from $5 to $125 , and notably, more affordable options are available than luxury ones, making it budget-friendly for consumers. The recommended brands consistently offer products with ratings of 3 or higher, indicating their high quality. The recommendation system focuses on providing a well-balanced selection of skincare items suitable for both dry and oily skin types, ensuring accessibility and affordability for a broad audience.
